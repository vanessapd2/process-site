Audience & Goals

The next step of the design challenge is to narrow down the audience that will use site and create specific goals the site will achieve.

Audience

I began by defining the audience involved and slowly narrowed it down into two major groups. It was seperated into the audience that Need Help and Supporters of Julie Valentine Center with a subgroup of Skeptics Julie Valentine Center. The Need Help group includes victims of sexual assault, child abuse, college and highschool aged girls, and those dealing with trauma. These are people who have been directly or personally impacted by sexual assault and child abuse. The other group of Supporters include resource groups like therapist, donors, churches, schools, government, etc. However, families, friends, people who don't have close relation, unbelievers of effectiveness, women who do not want to speak on the topic are under skeptics. Recognizing the audience helped guide the goals and future decision on about the redesign of the Julie Valentine Center website.

    Goals

    Overarching Goals

    Client
    • Provide immediate assistance
    • Support victims long-term by providing services
    • Partner with supporters
    Audience
    • Obtain the necessary help
    • Relieve trauma in a safe space
    • Understand the importance of this issue
    • Give monetary resources to the organization
    Website
    • To inform people on the issue and spread awareness
    • To allow people to donate and get involved
    • Share event information
    • Give monetary resources to the organization

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Teenage to adult woman who are victims of abuse Learn what the organization has to offer and how it can help them Become an active member in the organization. Persuade them to join and be involved in the services offered for help
The community close to victims Make them aware of the issue and have them advocate against abuse Inform them on how they can help and become active participants in this issue.
Those who want to volunteer and donate Inform them of duties, mission statement and goals Inform them of what they can do to help Increase donations, volunteers, encouragement, and most importantly awareness from the community
