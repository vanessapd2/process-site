Solution

The primary CTA for Julies Valentine Center is “Support.” The goal of the organization is to provide free-confidential service to victims of sexual assault and child abuse. The primary audience are men, women, or even children seeking assistance, guidance, or looking for research. For this reason, it is at the center under the organizations name, it draws the most attention because of the contrast between the image and the red of the button. The secondary CTAs are “Donate” and “Get Involved.” They are aimed the second audience, which are people who want to help by donating, volunteering, or simply to become more aware.
