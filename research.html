Research

To solve the design question, I conducted primary research about Julie Valentine Center and their current site. After, I gathered more information on sexual assualt and child abuse and other organizations who share a similar mission.

Primary Research

About Client

Julie Valentines Center is a private, non-profit organization that provides free, confidential services to victims of sexual assault and child abuse. The organization was founded by 13 female volunteers in 1974, when there were no support services for rape victims in Greenville. Over the years, Julie Valentines grew from their small office basement of Fourth Presbyterian Church, into a house, and now into their large house building off White Horse Road, thanks to the Alliance for Community Trust (ACT). As they've grown their mission remains the same; stop sexual violence and child abuse and the impact of these crimes through prevention, investigation, collaboration, treatment, and advocacy.

Current Site

As I’ve looked through the Julie Valentine Site. I’ve noticed they have a consistent color scheme, different platforms for users to access them, many resources, testimonials, and even a newsletter. However, the main issue I found was not curating the website to their main user, that is women looking for needed resources and services. Also, there is a lot of information that is not easy to read through because of the large amount of text. with. The branding is on the right track but could need a refresh to make the site and their other platforms cohesive. The navigation bar has many elements that could be condensed into 4 tabs. Overall, content can be organized by what is most important for the user groups and simplified to be easier to read and interact

Cultural Awareness

On Valentine's day, Julie Valentine held their annual event to showcase Survivors and Hometown Heroes journey to healing from child abuse, sexual assualt, and sex trafficking. The past 12 years, more than 1,000 partners, professionals, donors, and friends gather to support Julie Valentine Center financially and celebrate their work and survivors. Events like these help to draw more awareness in the area and how people can help those in these situations.

Solutions

Safe Harbor and Rainn are large organizations that work towards providing assistance and awareness on sexual assault. I think their websites are successful because both Safe Harbor and Rainn makes it clear their goal and where to go for immediate help by including phone number and get help CTAs.

Testimonals
