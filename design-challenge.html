Design Challenge

What am I solving?

The Problem

Provide accessible services and resource to victims of sexual assult and child abuse

The Design Question

How can the website easily show resources?

The Ultimate Impact

To assist women or families who are looking for help from sexual assault or child abuse.

Possible Solutions

Obvious Hierarchy: Services, Emergency number, mission, donation

Simple Navigation

Organization of information like phone number and location

Highlight most important information

Easy to Read

Context and Constraints

Female audience from different culture, ages, and backgrounds. Also, the women or family in need may be unable to make known their need for these resources.

The Final Design Question

How can the Julie Valentine site have their services and resources easily accessible for both those in need of help and looking to help?
