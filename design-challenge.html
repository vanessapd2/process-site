Design Challenge

The first step is to determine the design question using an exercise from the Human Centered Design Toolkit.

The Problem

Provide accessible services and resource to victims of sexual assult and child abuse.

The Design Question

How can the website easily show resources?

The Ultimate Impact

To assist women or families who are looking for help from sexual assault or child abuse.

Possible Solutions

  • Obvious Hierarchy: Services, Emergency number, mission, donation
  • Simple Navigation
  • Organization of information like phone number and location
  • Highlight most important information
  • Easy to read

Context and Contraints

  • Women, men and children from different culture, ages, and backgrounds.
  • Families in need may be unable to make known their need for these resources or even be aware of the resources offered.

The Final Design Question

How can the Julie Valentine site have their services and resources be easily accessible for both those in need of help and wanting to help?
