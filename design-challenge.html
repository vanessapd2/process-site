What is the problem you are trying to solve? Make the resources accessible and known to women Make the website user-friendly Concise and brief information

The Design Question How can the website easily show resources?

Possible Solutions Obvious Hierarchy: Services, Emergency number, mission, donation Simple Navigation Organization of information like phone number and location Highlight most important information Easy to Read

Context and Constraints Female audience from different culture, ages, and backgrounds. Also, the women or family in need may be unable to make known their need for these resources.
